ABOUT

Meet Deepthi, a visionary artist with a diverse creative journey. Hailing from the quaint town of Palakkad, India, her love for storytelling and aesthetics began to blossom early on. With a strong foundation in architecture, she discovered the perfect canvas to design mesmerizing worlds for films and narratives.

As an assistant art director, set designer, and set decorator, she lent her artistic touch to numerous Indian movie productions and captivating advertisements. Her keen eye for detail and ability to blend storytelling with spatial designs set her apart in the industry.

Her architectural background adds a unique dimension to her work, allowing her to craft spaces that not only look stunning but also serve the narrative flawlessly.

She is a certified scuba diver, avid hiker, and passionate travel enthusiast.These adventurous pursuits not only enrich her life but also fuel her creativity as a production designer, inspiring her to bring unique and captivating visual experiences to every project she works on.
