Assistant Art Director

Production: hungry man

Directed by Wayne McClammy

Production deisgn by Robb Buno

In Amazon’s Prime Day ad, LeBron James imagines life after basketball, exploring playful new careers like a hibachi chef, barber, and lounge singer. Each fantasy is sparked by deals he finds on the Amazon app, blending humor with aspiration. The ad’s core message is that “greatness is a deal away,” tying LeBron’s next chapter to the excitement of Prime Day shopping.

Shot on location in Los Angeles, CA and built on stages at Universal Studio
